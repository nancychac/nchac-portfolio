NICE TO MEET YOU

Hello! I'm Nancy, a UMass Lowell 2023 graduate transitioning from marketing analytics to a UX/UI designer. With a strong foundation in data analysis, branding, and a passion for user-centered design, I leverage my attained skills to create intuitive and engaging digital experiences.

My journey began with a fascination for different audiences understand and use data, but I soon realized that my true passion lies in designing solutions that not only meet user needs but also inspire delight. I thrive on collaboration, creativity, and the challenge of turning complex problems into seamless user journeys.

When I'm not designing, you can find me experimenting new tools that expand my skillset or geeking out on video games. I'm excited to blend my technical expertise with my love for design as I embark on this new chapter!
