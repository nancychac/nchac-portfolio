GRAPHIC DESIGN

Outside UX/UI, I take on graphic design projects. These range from my fellowship to freelancing. Here, you'll find a curated selection of my graphic design projects that showcase my versatility and creativity. From branding and logo design to promotional materials and digital illustrations, each project reflects my commitment to delivering visually compelling solutions tailored to client needs.

Explore the gallery to see how I transform ideas into impactful designs, and feel free to reach out if you’d like to collaborate or learn more about my process. Enjoy browsing!

UMASS ERP SUMMIT

Logo Design • Branding • Event Planning

An event hosted by UMass and Delloite to initiate discussions on improving ERP systems. I created the official logo and designed the event merchandise.

2024 UMPO LUNAR NEW YEAR EVENT

Flyer Design • Event Planning

A UMass President's Office event to celebrate the 2024 Lunar New Year. My role was graphic designer on the planning committee.

Strategic Planning Analytics and Reporting for Campuses (SPARC)

Logo Design • Branding • Copyright & Trademark

SPARC is a data analytics tool created by UMass for finance, enrollment, and procurement. I created the official logo and participated in the copyright and trademarking process of the design

GUAN (馆)

Branding • Logo Design

GUAN, meaning "palivion" in Mandarin Chinese, is a DJ group based in New York City, NY. The team consists of independent artists, SANDEL and BOYU. They hope to diversify the EDM scene and represent Asian American musicians. I developed the official logo.

SANDEL

Branding • Graphic Design

SANDEL is an independent DJ based in New York City, NY. I developed the logo and helped the artist with her branding on social media.
