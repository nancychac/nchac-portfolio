SANDEL

Social Media Marketing • Branding

PROJECT INFO

  • Tools: Canva
  • Role: Graphic Designer and Brand Manager
  • Project Type: Freelance

SANDEL is an independent DJ based in New York City. I supported the brand development of SANDEL. Specifically, created the logo and conceptualized the brand guide. This is a part-time project outside of my work.

PROCESS

PURPOSE

The artist requested to have a logo to establish her brand and create a solid foundation for her social media presence and positioning.

PLANNING & DESIGNING

  • The artist provided me with references to get inspiration from. The concept was minimalistic and clean. She also wanted her branding to be black and white.
  • I used the references she provided to create different iterations that resonate.
  • This brand guide helped establish her mission and positioning as a DJ.
  • BRAND GUIDE PDF

TAKEAWAYS

  • I was able to apply principles I learned from my social media marketing class to this assignment. I helped the artist recognize her branding and positioning as a DJ.
