sandel

Branding • Logo Design

  • Tools: Adobe Photoshop, Canva
  • Role: Graphic Designer
  • Project Type: Freelance

BACKGROUND

SANDEL is an independent DJ based in New York City. I supported the brand development of SANDEL. Specifically, created the logo and conceptualized the brand guide. This is a part-time project outside of my work. I was able to apply principles I learned from my social media marketing class to this assignment. I helped the artist recognize her branding and positioning as a DJ.

The artist requested to have a logo to establish her brand and create a solid foundation for her social media presence and positioning.

PLANNING & DESIGN

The artist provided me with references to get inspiration from. The concept was minimalistic and clean. She also wanted her branding to be black and white. I used the references she provided to create different iterations that resonate. 

I also helped curate a brand guide to provide direction on how she should brand position herself on social media.
