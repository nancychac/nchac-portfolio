ERP SUMMIT

Logo Design • Events

  • Tools: Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, FreePik
  • Team: UMPO Admin & Finance
  • Role: Graphic Designer
  • Project Type: Professional Work

BACKGROUND

ERP Summit is a joint event hosted by UMass and Deloitte. This was an opportunity to initiate discussions and learn more about how UMass can improve enterprise resource planning (ERP). My role was to design an event logo and web banner. This was also printed on free merchandise given to the attendees.

This was an event for initial discussions on upgrading UMass' ERP system. To exchange ideas and opinions with Deloitte for consultation. A logo was needed to demonstrate the mission of “Learn, Think, Share”

PLANNING & DESIGN

The team came up with different mockups of logos to potentially use. Here are the mockups I created. The team decided to the current version which is one of the samples I designed on Adobe Illustrator. Each icon represents how ERP helps the university. I also included the system's brand colors.

A website was created for attendees and viewers to stay engaged. I also designed the web banner which includes the square graphic and icons from the logos.

I worked together with the event coordinators to print the logos on stickers, pens, and notebooks.

REFLECTION

The main takeaway was to make sure that my graphics are easily scalable so they can adapt to various media. A problem that occurred was printing the pens. The printers had to accommodate only using the university logo and slogan due to the square graphic being too small for the pen. The colors also had to be changed so the graphics and text could be properly printed.
